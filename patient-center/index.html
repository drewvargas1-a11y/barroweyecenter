Helpful Tips

Making the Most of Your Visit

A little preparation goes a long way. Here's how to make your appointment as smooth as possible.

Bring Your Insurance Card

Have your vision or medical insurance card ready at check-in. We'll verify your benefits before your appointment.

Know Your Medications

Bring a list of current medications, including eye drops. Some medications can affect your vision and eye health.

Allow Extra Time

New patients should arrive 10–15 minutes early to complete intake paperwork, or download and fill out forms in advance.
