Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Darla S. Barrow, O.D.

A proud Logan County native, Dr. Barrow earned her Bachelor of Science in Biology and Chemistry from Murray State University before completing her Doctor of Optometry degree at Southern College of Optometry in 1998 — the same year she founded Barrow Eye Center.

Named Kentucky Young Optometrist of the Year in 2000–2001, Dr. Barrow has served on the Kentucky Optometric Board of Trustees and the Logan County Health Board. Her practice is built on the belief that every patient deserves personalized, compassionate care backed by the most advanced technology available.

"Your vision is our passion" isn't just a tagline — it's a commitment Dr. Barrow has honored for over 26 years in Russellville.
