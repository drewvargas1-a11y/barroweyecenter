What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive care for every stage of life — from your child's first exam to lifelong eye disease management.

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Full visual acuity testing, refraction, slit-lamp evaluation, glaucoma screening, retinal photography, and ophthalmoscopy — all under one roof.

Contact Lenses

Expert contact lens fittings for soft lenses, silicone hydrogel, and specialty contacts. Reorder online through our convenient patient store.

Order Contacts Online →

Eyewear & Frames

An extensive selection of designer frames and precision-crafted lenses to fit your style, prescription, and budget perfectly.

Glaucoma Management

Early detection and ongoing monitoring of glaucoma with tonometry and optic nerve assessment. Referrals for laser and microsurgery when needed.

Macular Degeneration

Early AMD detection using AdaptDx® dark adaptation testing — the gold standard in identifying AMD before vision loss begins.

Diabetic Eye Care

Specialized screenings for diabetic retinopathy using Optomap® ultra-widefield retinal imaging to detect complications early.

Expert Guidance

Our team helps you find the perfect fit — from style to prescription.
